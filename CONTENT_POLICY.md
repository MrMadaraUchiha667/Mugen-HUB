# Content Policy

MUGEN-HUB is a community-driven platform.

## Ownership

Every creator retains ownership of the content they upload.

Examples include:

- Characters
- Stages
- Screenpacks
- Lifebars
- Portrait Packs
- Music Packs
- Full Games
- Artwork

---

## Upload Requirements

By uploading content you declare that:

- You are the original creator

OR

- You have permission to redistribute it.

---

## Prohibited Content

The following content may be removed:

- Malware
- Viruses
- Illegal material
- Copyright infringement
- Hate speech
- NSFW material
- Spam

---

## DMCA

If you are the copyright owner and believe your work has been uploaded without permission, please open an issue or contact the project maintainer.

The content will be reviewed and removed when appropriate.

---

## AI Generated Content

AI-assisted content must clearly indicate that artificial intelligence was used during creation.

---

## Preservation

One of the goals of MUGEN-HUB is digital preservation of the M.U.G.E.N community while respecting the rights of original creators.
